Teriyaki Chicken Recipe

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts: use boneless skinless chicken.
  • Olive oil or vegetable oil
  • Soy sauce: use 2 1/2 tablespoon standard soy sauce with 1 1/2 tablespoon water.
  • Brown sugar
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sesame oil
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Cornstarch
  • Sesame seeds and green onions

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a large skillet or wok.
  2. Add the chicken and let the chicken cook until slightly browned on the bottom. Then flip chicken pieces over and cook through.
  3. While the chicken cooks, whisk together the teriyaki sauce mixture.
  4. Pour the sauce into the skillet once the chicken is nearly finished cooking through. Let the sauce cook and simmer until thickened.
  5. Optionally, spread the sesame seeds and green onions.
  6. Enjoy your meal! The teriyaki chicken can be served with brown rice, white rice, or steamed vegetables.
