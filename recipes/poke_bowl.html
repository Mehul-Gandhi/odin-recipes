Poke Bowl Recipe

Ingredients

  • Tuna
  • Salmon
  • Chicken
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 1/4 cup light mayo
  • 1 tbsp sriracha
  • 4 cups cooked white or brown rice
  • 1 cup diced cucumber
  • 1/2 cup shredded carrots
  • 1/2 cup grilled onions
  • 1/2 cup pineapple
  • 2 large avocados, peeled and sliced
  • 1 tbsp black sesame seeds
  • 2 Seaweed Slices

Steps

  1. Cook chicken and rice.
  2. Use a sharp knife to cut tuna, salmon, and chicken into a dice.
  3. Add meat, soy sauce, sesae oil, and rice vinegar to a medium-sized bowl. Toss to combine.
  4. Add mayo and sriracha sauce to a bowl. Stir to combine. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Divide the cooked rice between four bowls. Spoon the different meats on different sides of the bowl.
  6. Surround bowl with a pile of cucumber, pineapple, and carrots. Spread half of an avocado on top of the bowl.
  7. Drizzle the spicy mayo over the bowl. Sprinkle with grilled onions and sesame seeds.
  8. Enjoy your meal!
