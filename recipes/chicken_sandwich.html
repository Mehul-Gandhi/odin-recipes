Chicken Sandwich

A simple chicken sandwich recipe with essential ingredients.

Ingredients

  • Two bread slices
  • 1 Chicken Patty (Costco)
  • Ketchup
  • Mayonnaise
  • Tomato Slices
  • Onion Slices
  • Cheese Slice
  • Lettuce

Steps

  1. Cook the chicken patty in the oven for about 15 minutes or in the air fryer for about 10 minutes.
  2. Toast the bread.
  3. Spread ketchup and mayonaisse on both bread slices.
  4. Cut tomatoes and onions into slices. Put the slices on one bread slice evenly.
  5. Place the chicken patty right above the tomato and onion slices.
  6. Place the cheese above the chicken patty.
  7. Place the lettuce above the cheese.
  8. Close the sandwich with the other bread slice.
  9. Enjoy your meal!
